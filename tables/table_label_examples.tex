\texttt{M3} instructing & ``Run the failing case, print the parser output, add a guard for empty fields, then rerun the test.'' & Explicit ordered steps tell the user what actions to take and in what sequence. \\

\texttt{M6} questioning & ``Which constraint matters more here: preserving tone or shortening the paragraph?'' & A question that advances problem solving or reflection. \\
